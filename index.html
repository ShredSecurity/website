Frequently Asked Questions

What is a smart contract security audit?

A smart contract security audit is a comprehensive review of blockchain code to identify vulnerabilities, logic flaws, and potential exploits. Our team examines every line of code using both automated tools and manual analysis to ensure your smart contracts are secure before deployment.

How long does a security audit take?

Audit duration depends on the complexity of your project. Simple contracts typically take 1-2 weeks, while complex DeFi protocols may require 3-6 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during our initial assessment and keep you updated throughout the process.

What types of vulnerabilities do you find?

We identify critical, high, medium, and low severity vulnerabilities including reentrancy attacks, integer overflow/underflow, access control issues, logic flaws, gas optimization problems, and compliance violations. Each finding includes detailed explanations and remediation steps.

Do you provide post-audit support?

Yes, we provide ongoing support after the audit. This includes code review guidance for implementing fixes, best practices recommendations, and re-auditing critical functions to ensure vulnerabilities are properly addressed before deployment.

What makes Shred Security different?

Our team combines deep blockchain expertise with cutting-edge security research. We've audited hundreds of smart contracts and helped secure billions in digital assets. Our 99.9% vulnerability detection rate and comprehensive reporting set us apart in the industry.

How much does a security audit cost?

Audit costs vary based on project complexity, code size, and scope. We provide transparent, customized pricing after reviewing your requirements. Contact us for a detailed quote tailored to your specific project needs.
